3. What the App Does

Untraced is a permission auditing tool that scans apps installed on your Android device and identifies which ones have been granted location, microphone, and camera permissions. The App provides risk indicators based on a curated on-device database to help you make informed decisions about your app permissions.

All scanning, analysis, and data storage occur entirely on your device. Untraced has no server-side component. No data about you or your device is collected, transmitted, or shared. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
